Models of Human Physiology

Whether it's engineering the next generation of human-centric products, developing safer protective gear, or designing a more efficient physical training program, models of human physiology can provide you with new insights, while limiting the need for resource-intensive experimental testing.

A Lack of Appropriate Models

However, current methods for modeling human physiology are severely limited. They often rely on purely statistically derived and isolated physiological principles.
